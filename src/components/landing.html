Our Story

Founder, Jonathan Li, shares a passion for
Board Games, Boba, and Delicious Food.

He combined them all to become Sip & Play, Park Slope's first board game cafe.
It is a straightforward concept, come in with your friends and family to play any board game from our library of 300+ games!
We hope when you visit, you also enjoy our coffee, espresso, boba, sandwiches, and snacks!

Make sure not to miss out on our diverse menu offerings, where you can find a wide range of everyone's favorite snacks and drinks to satisfy your cravings while you are playing our board games.

Our Game Collection

We have an incredible selection of over 300+ board games at our cafe! Come by to enjoy your favorite drinks and snacks while having an amazing time with your friends and family. It's a perfect place to relax, have fun, and create joyful memories together.

Upcoming Events

Be sure not to miss out on our upcoming events at the cafe, where you'll have the opportunity to socialize and connect with new friends while creating wonderful memories with your loved ones. You can look forward to savoring beverages and snacks in a lively and enjoyable atmosphere.
